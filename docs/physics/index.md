Notes on physics — from classical mechanics to quantum field theory.

- **[Mechanics](/physics/mechanics/intro)** — statics and dynamics of rigid bodies
- **[Signals & Systems](/physics/signals-systems/intro)** — continuous- and discrete-time signal analysis

- **[Quantum Field Theory](/physics/quantum-field-theory/intro)** — QFT in a nutshell
- **[Dynamical Systems & Simulation](/physics/simulation/intro)** — MATLAB/Simulink-based numerical modeling
