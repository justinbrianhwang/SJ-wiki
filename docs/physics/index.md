# Physics

Notes on physics — from classical mechanics to quantum field theory.

## Topics

- **[General Physics](/physics/general/intro)** — kinematics, dynamics, waves, thermodynamics, electromagnetism (HRW)
- **[Mechanics (Statics & Dynamics)](/physics/mechanics/intro)** — statics and dynamics of rigid bodies
- **[Electromagnetics](/physics/electromagnetics/intro)** — Maxwell, transmission lines, waves, antennas (Ulaby)
- **[Signals & Systems](/physics/signals-systems/intro)** — continuous- and discrete-time signal analysis (Oppenheim)
- **[Quantum Mechanics](/physics/quantum-mechanics/intro)** — the standard quantum theory (Sakurai + Ballentine + Gottfried + Schiff)
- **[Quantum Field Theory](/physics/quantum-field-theory/intro)** — QFT in a nutshell (Zee)
- **[Dynamical Systems & Simulation](/physics/simulation/intro)** — MATLAB/Simulink-based numerical modeling

The quantum mechanics section cross-references **Sakurai**, **Ballentine**, **Gottfried**, and **Schiff** — comparing formalisms rather than picking one canonical treatment.
